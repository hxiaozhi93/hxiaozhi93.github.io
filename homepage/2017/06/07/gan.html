生成对抗网络

  • GAN

理论相关：

（GAN）生成式对抗网络：Goodfellow I J, Pougetabadie J, Mirza M, et al. Generative Adversarial Nets[J]. Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems, 2014, 3:2672-2680.

（CGAN）条件生成式对抗网络：Mirza M, Osindero S. Conditional Generative Adversarial Nets[J]. Computer Science, 2014:2672-2680

（LAPGAN）拉普拉斯金字塔生成式对抗网络：Denton E, Chintala S, Szlam A, et al. Deep Generative Image Models using a Laplacian Pyramid of Adversarial Networks[J]. Computer Science, 2015.

（f-GAN）（以第三方角度看待GAN，从距离度量的角度出发）（(NIPS-2016)）：Nowozin S, Cseke B, Tomioka R. f-GAN: Training Generative Neural Samplers using Variational Divergence Minimization[J]. 2016.

（EBGAN）（以第三方角度看待GAN，从能量模型的角度出发）（ICLR-2017）：Zhao J, Mathieu M, Lecun Y. Energy-based Generative Adversarial Network[J]. 2016.

（InfoGAN）（从GAN网络本身出发而对GAN进行改进）（(NIPS-2016)）：Chen X, Duan Y, Houthooft R, et al. InfoGAN: Interpretable Representation Learning by Information Maximizing Generative Adversarial Nets[J]. 2016

（DCGAN）深度卷积GAN (ICLR-2016)：Radford A, Metz L, Chintala S. Unsupervised Representation Learning with Deep Convolutional Generative Adversarial Networks[J]. Computer Science, 2015.

（WGAN）：Arjovsky M, Chintala S, Bottou L. Wasserstein GAN[J]. 2017.

Improved GAN (NIPS-2016 workshop)：Salimans T, Goodfellow I, Zaremba W, et al. Improved Techniques for Training GANs[J]. 2016.

针对GAN训练过程的不稳定问题， Soumith等人在github上专门总结了一个文档：https://github.com/soumith/ganhacks ：How to Train a GAN? Tips and tricks to make GANs work

GAN应用：

利用GANs把文字转化为图像：Reed S, Akata Z, Yan X, et al. Generative Adversarial Text to Image Synthesis[J]. 2016.

（StackGAN）文字转化为图像：Zhang H, Xu T, Li H, et al. StackGAN: Text to Photo-realistic Image Synthesis with Stacked Generative Adversarial Networks[J]. 2016.

GANs应用于超分辨率：Ledig C, Theis L, Huszar F, et al. Photo-Realistic Single Image Super-Resolution Using a Generative Adversarial Network[J]. 2016.

自动面部老化：Antipov G, Baccouche M, Dugelay J L. Face Aging With Conditional Generative Adversarial Networks[J]. 2017.

（Image-to-Image Translation）：Image-to-Image Translation with Conditional Adversarial Networks

（Perceptual Similarity Metrics）（GAN在应用层面的改进）（(NIPS-2016)）： Dosovitskiy A, Brox T. Generating Images with Perceptual Similarity Metrics based on Deep Networks[J]. 2016.
